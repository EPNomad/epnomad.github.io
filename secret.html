What is Nomad?

Nomad is a product recognition API built by developers for developers. We are able to recognize over 10,000 models and we allow developers to train their own models. Not only that, but our models are run on GPUs allowing us to soar over the speeds of our competitors. What would have taken 15 seconds on a typical CPU to run now takes 0.10 seconds on Nomad's revolutionary new platform. Nomad was bred and raised in AngelHack's Hackcelerator program where we won 4th place in their Global Demo Day. Now, we are raising capital from investors and looking for interns.
